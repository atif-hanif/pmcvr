PRINCE2®

At PMC we have pioneered the release of PRINCE2® within a VR environment. PRINCE2® training is based on the PRINCE2® 6th Edition. We deliver both the Foundation (PRINCE2® 6th Edition Foundation) and the Practitioner (PRINCE2® 6th Edition Practitioner) training. All our training includes exam vouchers in the training package.

Using a VR Hub

A VR Hub is simply a small quiet and usually dedicated training space or meeting room in your building where you can use your VR headset in peace and quiet and travel to your game zone and learn. Play games and learn through experiencing simulated projects.

Setting up a VR Hub

PMC can help you develop your VR Hub in your training or office environment. All you need is a small dedicated space, and we’ll do the rest to set up the VR headsets and environment.

A Face-to-face course involves accessing VR Games, masterclasses face to face and accessing guided content while a structure timetable.
